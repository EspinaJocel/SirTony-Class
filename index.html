Members of 3YE under GH Entertainment

Son Yu Ji (손유지)

Yuji (유지) is a South Korean singer under GH Entertainment.

She is the leader of the girl group 3YE and a member of the co-ed group Triple7. She is also a former member of Apple.B. Yuji originally debuted as a member of Apple.B on August 1, 2017 with the group's first digital single "Woochuchu". In October, she became a contestant on the survival show The Unit. She was eliminated in episode 7 after ranking 59th place.

About Us

3YE Profile and Facts. 3YE (써드아이) is a 3-member girl group under GH Entertainment. The group consists of: Yuji, Yurim, and Haeun. They debuted on May 21st, 2019 with DMT (Do Ma Thang)

3YE HISTORY

All 3YE members were a part of the five member girl group Apple.B, which disbanded after two of the members left. The remaining girls: Yuji, Yurim, and Haeun later re-debuted as 3YE with the digital single "DTM". In September 17, 2019, they came back with their second digital single "OOMM" . 3YE released their third digital single "Queen" on February 21, 2020.

Testimonials

the BIAS List

I wasn’t too enthralled with 3YE’s debut year sound, but February’s Queen hit me unexpectedly hard. I appreciated how it drew from older K-pop elements, largely foregoing trends. That song alone made me anticipate the group’s eventual comeback. For the first time, a 3YE track arrived with expectations. And judging from my initial shoulder shrug reaction to YESSIR, expectations can be a dangerous thing.
In reality, I still prefer YESSIR to 3YE’s 2019 material, but it loses some of the fun electro spark that made Queen such a treat. Instead, the song feels like a spiritual successor to Everglow’s Dun Dun, given a brassy makeover to compliment the year’s trends. YESSIR is loud… pretty much from beginning to end.
